Tybalt Capulet

By Raman Baig

A burning iron, forged in the fires of hell

Against everyone, quite the rebel

A rage, held back by only a few

Igniting a dormant battle as new

Darkness, the sap in its veins

When it sees peace, it pains

Impatient, the cause of all hate

And for that duo, it ends someone’s fate

A force takes revenge, giving the metal a title, ‘late’

But the ferric still chooses what’s on its plate

Against hamsters, who get trapped in a cage

And empties their souls, their life gauge

There is no calm in its presence

And those who retaliate, eradicated will be their essence
