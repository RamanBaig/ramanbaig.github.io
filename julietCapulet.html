Juliet Capulet

By Raman Baig

A fragile entity, one which is hard to understand

A flower, sweetly transforming a garden with its brand

Infatuated, attracted to anyone who lends out their hand

Just like the love she was shown by land

Indecisive, like a bee without its stinger

Or a grounded body, whose soul does linger

Covered eyes, trapped by the view of the mirror

View of a cat, chasing and meeting the killer

The sorrowful death of the lover

A tragic ending to her whisper

Don’t think we’ve forgotten, about you

For it was the soil who wanted their due
